Glossary
--------

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 2

.. glossary::

   Adam
      A SGD-based optimization algorithm. See :cite:`2015:kingma` for details.

   ADMM
      The alternating direction method of multipliers (ADMM) algorithm. See :cite:`2011:boyd` for details.

   BFGS
      The Broyden-Fletcher-Goldfarb-Shanno (BFGS) algorithm.

   IIS
      The improved iterative scaling (IIS) algorithm.

   MaxEnt
      The maximum entropy (MaxEnt) model.
